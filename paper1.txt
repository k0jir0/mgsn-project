ABSTRACT
--------
This paper describes the design and operation of the MGSN Strategy Engine, a
browser-based autonomous decision system deployed as an ICP asset canister at
yezrb-diaaa-aaaah-qugnq-cai.icp0.io. The engine ingests 22 months of real
on-chain market data, live prices from the ICPSwap DEX, and ICP/USD spot rates
to produce a composite buy/sell/hold signal, optimal position sizing, and
multi-scenario portfolio analysis for the MGSN token. It also calculates
liquidity provision yield, impermanent loss, and LP fee compounding projections
for the MGSN/ICP pool on ICPSwap. No server-side code, external APIs owned by
us, or custodial keys are required; the user's wallet executes all trades
directly on ICPSwap.


1. BACKGROUND AND MOTIVATION
-----------------------------
MGSN is a low-cap token on the Internet Computer (canister ID:
mgsn7-iiaaa-aaaag-qjvsa-cai). Its market cap is a small fraction of the BOB
token ecosystem (BOB canister: 7pail-xaaaa-aaaas-aabmq-cai), which in turn
trades against ICP. Because MGSN has limited independent price history, its
fair value is most legibly read through the lens of ecosystem momentum (BOB
price series) and relative cost (MGSN priced in ICP).

The goal of the engine is to answer three questions at any given moment:
  1. Should I buy, hold, or reduce my MGSN position right now?
  2. How large should each position entry be relative to my liquid capital?
  3. What does liquidity provision in the MGSN/ICP pool return after fees and
     impermanent loss?

These questions are addressed through a six-factor composite signal, a Kelly
Criterion position sizer, a 22-month backtest, and an LP yield + compound
projector.


2. DATA SOURCES
---------------
The engine uses three data feeds:

  a) Historical timeline (22 months: July 2024 вЂ“ April 2026)
     Stored in demoData.js. Contains monthly snapshots of:
       - ICP/USD (real, sourced from Binance/CoinGecko)
       - BOB/USD (real, sourced from ICPSwap TokenStorage canister)
       - MGSN/USD (current price applied uniformly; no stored history yet)
       - BOB trading volume per month
       - MGSN and BOB token supply (fixed)

  b) Live ICP/USD spot price
     Fetched from the CoinGecko public API on page load and every 60 seconds:
       GET https://api.coingecko.com/api/v3/simple/price?ids=internet-computer&vs_currencies=usd

  c) Live MGSN/USD and BOB/USD prices from ICPSwap
     Fetched from the ICPSwap NodeIndex canister on ICP mainnet:
       GET https://ggzvv-5qaaa-aaaag-qck7a-cai.raw.icp0.io/getAllTokens
     This returns the current USD price for every token on ICPSwap, including
     MGSN and BOB. Pool statistics (24h volume, TVL) are fetched from:
       GET https://uvevg-iyaaa-aaaak-ac27q-cai.raw.icp0.io/token/list

All three fetches run in parallel (Promise.allSettled) on bootstrap and every
60 seconds thereafter. The engine degrades gracefully to historical data if any
live feed fails.


3. THE SIX-FACTOR COMPOSITE SIGNAL
------------------------------------
Each factor scores the current market state on a 0вЂ“100 scale where 0 = strong
sell and 100 = strong buy. The composite is a weighted average of all six.

  Signal 1 вЂ” BOB RSI (5-month, Wilder method)
  Weight: 20%
  Wilder's Relative Strength Index applied to the 22-month BOB price series
  using a 5-period lookback (appropriate for monthly data). Thresholds:
    RSI < 30  в†’ score 90 (oversold, high reversal probability)
    RSI < 40  в†’ score 75 (accumulation zone)
    RSI < 50  в†’ score 60 (mildly bearish)
    RSI < 60  в†’ score 40 (mildly bullish)
    RSI < 70  в†’ score 25 (elevated, reduce new buys)
    RSI в‰Ґ 70  в†’ score 10 (overbought)

  Signal 2 вЂ” BOB SMA Crossover (3-month fast / 8-month slow)
  Weight: 15%
  Simple moving average of BOB price on two periods. The spread ((fastв€’slow)/slow)
  determines direction and magnitude:
    Spread < в€’15%  в†’ score 90 (deep bear cross вЂ” prime accumulation)
    Spread < в€’5%   в†’ score 70 (bear cross)
    Spread < 0%    в†’ score 55 (approaching cross)
    Spread < +5%   в†’ score 45 (mild bull)
    Spread < +15%  в†’ score 30 (bull cross вЂ” slow entry)
    Spread в‰Ґ +15%  в†’ score 15 (strong bull вЂ” late entry)

  Signal 3 вЂ” mNAV Ratio
  Weight: 20%
  The market-NAV ratio is defined as: mNAV = (MGSN market cap) / (BOB market cap)
  = (MGSN price Г— MGSN supply) / (BOB price Г— BOB supply).
  This is compared against its 22-month historical average. A deeply discounted
  ratio (MGSN undervalued relative to BOB ecosystem size) produces a high score:
    mNAV < avg в€’ 50%  в†’ score 90
    mNAV < avg в€’ 20%  в†’ score 70
    near avg          в†’ score 50
    mNAV > avg + 40%  в†’ score 10

  Signal 4 вЂ” MGSN/ICP Relative Cost Basis
  Weight: 15%
  The price of MGSN expressed in ICP (MGSN/ICP) compared against its 22-month
  average. When MGSN is historically cheap relative to ICP вЂ” i.e., buying MGSN
  with ICP costs less than it has historically вЂ” the score rises:
    Dev < в€’60%  в†’ score 95 (very cheap vs ICP)
    Dev < в€’30%  в†’ score 75
    Dev < 0%    в†’ score 55
    Dev < +30%  в†’ score 35
    Dev в‰Ґ +30%  в†’ score 15

  Signal 5 вЂ” MACD on BOB (3/8/5 monthly)
  Weight: 15%
  MACD computed with EMA(3) в€’ EMA(8), signal line EMA(5), optimised for monthly
  data. The histogram direction (current в€’ previous) is the primary input:
    Histogram < 0 and turning up  в†’ score 80 (early divergence вЂ” strongest entry)
    Histogram < 0 and falling     в†’ score 70 (trending down but accumulation context)
    Histogram > 0 and turning down в†’ score 35 (fading momentum вЂ” slow new buys)
    Histogram > 0 and rising      в†’ score 25 (late-stage bull вЂ” expensive)

  Signal 6 вЂ” Bollinger Band %B on BOB (8-period, 2Пѓ)
  Weight: 15%
  Bollinger Bands computed on the 22-month BOB series. %B places the current
  price within the band: %B = (price в€’ lower) / (upper в€’ lower). Values near 0
  mean the price is near the lower band (historically high-probability reversal):
    %B < 5%   в†’ score 92 (below lower band вЂ” extreme compression)
    %B < 20%  в†’ score 78 (lower zone)
    %B < 40%  в†’ score 62 (below midline)
    %B < 60%  в†’ score 50 (at midline)
    %B < 80%  в†’ score 35 (above midline)
    %B < 95%  в†’ score 22 (near upper band)
    %B в‰Ґ 95%  в†’ score 8  (above upper band вЂ” overbought)

  Composite = RSIГ—0.20 + SMAГ—0.15 + mNAVГ—0.20 + ICPValГ—0.15 + MACDГ—0.15 + BBГ—0.15

  Action thresholds:
    Composite в‰Ґ 75  в†’ STRONG BUY
    Composite в‰Ґ 62  в†’ BUY
    Composite в‰Ґ 50  в†’ ACCUMULATE
    Composite в‰Ґ 38  в†’ HOLD
    Composite < 38  в†’ REDUCE


4. KELLY CRITERION POSITION SIZING
------------------------------------
After producing the composite signal, the engine calculates the mathematically
optimal fraction of liquid capital to deploy per entry using the Kelly Criterion:

  f* = (p Г— b в€’ q) / b

  where:
    p = historical win rate of BOB monthly returns (closes above prior month)
    q = 1 в€’ p (loss rate)
    b = average monthly gain / average monthly loss ratio

Applied to the 22-month BOB return series, this produces a full-Kelly fraction.
Because Kelly fractions are theoretically optimal but practically volatile, the
engine applies quarter-Kelly (f*/4), capped at 25% of liquid capital per entry.

Example at April 2026 values (p в‰€ 0.55, b в‰€ 1.4):
  Full Kelly в‰€ 16%
  Quarter-Kelly в‰€ 4%
  Interpretation: deploy 4% of liquid crypto capital per MGSN entry.

The engine displays this as "X% of portfolio per entry" alongside a brief note
showing the win rate and W/L ratio from the 22-month sample.

6. ARBITRAGE DETECTOR
----------------------
The engine cross-references two price estimates of MGSN:
  1. ICPSwap live price вЂ” fetched from the NodeIndex canister in real time.
  2. BOB-implied price вЂ” the current MGSN price scaled by the BOB price ratio
     between now and the most recent historical data point.
     Formula: mgsnImplied = mgsnHistorical Г— (bobLive / bobHistorical)

If the ICPSwap live price deviates from the BOB-implied price by more than 10%:
  - Price < implied by >10%  в†’ "BUY SIGNAL" (MGSN undervalued vs BOB momentum)
  - Price > implied by >10%  в†’ "SELL / LP" (MGSN overvalued vs BOB momentum)

This reflects the thesis that MGSN and BOB trade with broad correlation due to
shared ecosystem liquidity on ICPSwap. Short-term divergences represent
exploitable relative-value trades.


7. DCA BACKTEST ENGINE
-----------------------
The backtest engine simulates three 22-month investment strategies on the
historical timeline and a fourth projected from current date:

  Strategy A вЂ” Signal DCA
  Monthly allocation is multiplied by a composite multiplier (0.25Г—вЂ“2.5Г—)
  derived from four of the six signals: RSI, SMA crossover, MACD histogram,
  and Bollinger %B. Each signal contributes a sub-multiplier:
    RSI:  < 35 в†’ 2Г—, < 50 в†’ 1.5Г—, > 65 в†’ 0.5Г—
    SMA:  spread < в€’10% в†’ 2Г—, < 0% в†’ 1.5Г—, > 10% в†’ 0.5Г—
    MACD: histogram < 0 в†’ 1.5Г—, в‰Ґ 0 в†’ 0.75Г—
    BB%B: < 0.20 в†’ 1.5Г—, > 0.80 в†’ 0.6Г—
  Composite multiplier = RSIГ—0.3 + SMAГ—0.3 + MACDГ—0.2 + BBГ—0.2

  Strategy B вЂ” Flat DCA
  Fixed allocation of $N per month regardless of market conditions.

  Strategy C вЂ” Lump Sum
  Total budget deployed on day one.

MGSN price is projected throughout the backtest by scaling from the current
MGSN/BOB ratio: mgsnProjected[t] = mgsnNow Г— (bobPrice[t] / bobNow). This
uses the BOB price series as a proxy for MGSN trajectory.

Results displayed: final portfolio value, total invested, ROI, and a Chart.js
line chart with all three portfolio value series overlaid.


8. LP YIELD CALCULATOR AND COMPOUND PROJECTOR
-----------------------------------------------
The engine models expected returns from providing liquidity in the MGSN/ICP
pool on ICPSwap (0.3% fee tier).

  Fee yield estimate:
    Pool share = userDeposit / (poolTVL + userDeposit)
    Annual fees = monthlyVolume Г— 0.003 Г— 12 Г— poolShare
  
  Volume is sourced from the live ICPSwap pool stats API if available; otherwise
  a conservative proxy is used (5% of average monthly BOB volume).

  Three scenarios are computed:
    Conservative: 40% of estimated monthly volume
    Base:         estimated monthly volume
    Optimistic:   3Г— estimated monthly volume

  Impermanent loss table:
  The standard IL formula is applied across five MGSN/ICP price divergence
  scenarios: в€’75%, в€’50%, no change, +100%, +300%.
    IL(r) = (2в€љr / (1 + r)) в€’ 1   where r = new price ratio / initial price ratio

  Compound projection:
  Fee income from the base scenario is modelled as monthly reinvestment over
  12, 24, and 36 months. The compound growth curve is rendered on a Chart.js
  line chart.

11. ARCHITECTURE
-----------------
The engine is a single JavaScript module (src/strategy.js, ~1,425 lines) bundled
by Vite 7.x into a static asset (dist/strategy-*.js, ~54 KB gzip: 15 KB).
It is served from strategy.html, a second entry point added to vite.config.js
alongside the existing dashboard (index.html).

No backend canister changes were required. All computation runs in the browser.
The ICP asset canister (frontend: yezrb-diaaa-aaaah-qugnq-cai) serves both
HTML pages. The Content-Security-Policy in public/.ic-assets.json5 was updated
to allow outbound fetch to:
  - https://api.coingecko.com          (ICP/USD spot price)
  - https://ggzvv-5qaaa-aaaag-qck7a-cai.raw.icp0.io  (ICPSwap NodeIndex)
  - https://uvevg-iyaaa-aaaak-ac27q-cai.raw.icp0.io  (ICPSwap pool stats)

Navigation between the dashboard and strategy engine is handled by a two-link
nav bar added to both pages.


12. LIMITATIONS AND FUTURE WORK
---------------------------------
  a) MGSN price history
     MGSN has no stored on-chain price history as of April 2026. All backtesting
     uses BOB as a proxy. As MGSN builds more trading history on ICPSwap, the
     engine can be updated to use MGSN price series directly for RSI and BB.

  b) Signal sample size
     22 monthly data points is a small sample for technical indicators. RSI and
     MACD are particularly sensitive to early data weights at this sample size.
     As the dataset grows, the signals will become more statistically robust.

  c) Kelly Criterion limitations
     The Kelly fraction is computed from the BOB monthly return distribution,
     not from MGSN directly. The distribution assumption is that monthly closes
     represent independent Bernoulli trials, which understates serial correlation
     in crypto price series. Quarter-Kelly is applied as a safety margin.

  d) LP modelling
     Volume estimates use BOB as a proxy at a 5% ratio. Actual MGSN pool volumes
     can differ significantly. The live ICPSwap pool stats API will correct this
     when the pool accumulates sufficient liquidity and volume data.

  e) Automation
     The current engine is a decision-support tool; actual trades must be
     executed manually on ICPSwap. A future version could trigger automated
     orders via ICPSwap's on-chain swap interface using Internet Identity
     authentication and ICP agent calls.


APPENDIX вЂ” Signal weight rationale
------------------------------------
  RSI    20% вЂ” Primary momentum oscillator; most reliable indicator at small sample sizes
  mNAV   20% вЂ” Fundamental valuation; MGSN/BOB market cap ratio is the clearest value signal
  SMA    15% вЂ” Trend direction; supports RSI in confirming bear/bull phase
  ICP    15% вЂ” Relative entry cost; ICP-denominated price anchors real purchasing power
  MACD   15% вЂ” Momentum acceleration; leading indicator for reversals
  BB%B   15% вЂ” Mean reversion; probability-based band position signal

All six signals are directionally consistent: high scores indicate a buy
environment and low scores indicate a sell or wait environment. The weights
reflect the relative reliability and independence of each signal given the
22-month sample of monthly data.
